# Glossary & Terminology

Comprehensive reference for acronyms, terms, and platform definitions used in RDC marketing analytics.

## Business Model

### Two-Sided Marketplace

Our business operates a two-sided marketplace connecting two distinct groups:

**Consumers**
- Individuals interested in buying, selling, or renting a home
- Also referred to as "Leads" when they submit their information
- Marketing efforts are aimed at attracting these users

**Customers**
- Real estate agents, brokerages, and Realtors
- Use our service to connect with Consumers
- Pay for connections with motivated Consumers

**The Connection**
- Revenue is generated by successfully connecting motivated Consumers with Customers
- Revenue model is primarily success-based (referral fees on closed deals)
- Our success is directly tied to our Customers' success in closing deals
- **Lead quality is paramount** due to success-based revenue model

## Key Metrics

### Revenue & Value Metrics

| Metric | Full Name | Definition | Formula |
|--------|-----------|------------|---------|
| **EFR** | Expected Future Revenue | The expected revenue generated by a lead submission. **North Star metric.** | - |
| **RPL** | Revenue Per Lead | Expected future revenue generated per lead | `SUM(EFR) / SUM(LEADS)` |
| **ROAS** | Return on Ad Spend | Profitability of advertising campaigns. Always calculate using EFR. | `EFR / Spend` |

**ROAS Interpretation:**
- ROAS > 1: Campaigns generating more revenue than cost
- ROAS = 1: Campaigns breaking even
- ROAS < 1: Campaigns losing money

### Cost & Efficiency Metrics

| Metric | Full Name | Definition | Formula |
|--------|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Spend** | Advertising Spend | Total monetary amount invested in advertising campaigns. Also: Ad Spend, Marketing Cost | - |
| **CPL** | Cost Per Lead | Cost incurred to acquire each lead | `SUM(Spend) / SUM(LEADS)` |
| **CPC** | Cost Per Click | Cost incurred for each click on an ad | `SUM(Spend) / SUM(Clicks)` |

### Conversion & Quality Metrics

| Metric | Full Name | Definition | Formula |
|--------|-----------|------------|---------|
| **LSR** | Lead Submission Rate | Rate at which ad clicks convert into leads | `SUM(Leads) / SUM(Clicks)` |
| **Good Quality Ratio** | - | Proportion of sell-intent leads classified as "good quality" | `SUM(GQ_SELL_LEADS) / SUM(SELL_INTENT_LEADS)` |
| **Sell Leads Ratio** | - | Proportion of total leads with "sell intent" | `SUM(SELL_INTENT_LEADS) / SUM(LEADS)` |

### Audience Metrics

| Metric | Full Name | Definition |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **UU** | Unique Users | Key audience growth metric, typically measured YoY |
| **Median Home Value** | - | Measure of traffic quality; higher values indicate higher-value traffic |

## External Factors

### Seasonal Trends
- **Slower Periods**: Winter months and major holidays see reduced activity
- **Peak Seasons**: Spring and summer are busiest for buying, selling, and moving
- **Impact**: Lead volume fluctuates predictably throughout the year

### Macroeconomic Trends
Key indicators that directly impact real estate market activity:
- **Mortgage interest rates**: Higher rates reduce affordability and transaction volume
- **Consumer confidence**: Affects willingness to make major purchases
- **Economic uncertainty**: Can delay buying/selling decisions

### Competitive Activity
- Market for real estate leads is **finite and highly competitive**
- We compete with other businesses for a limited pool of potential clients
- Increased competitive spend can impact our costs and lead volume
- Monitor for shifts in market share
